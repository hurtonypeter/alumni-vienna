What is QFin?

The Quantitative Finance Club (QFin) is a club for alumni and current students of the master programm Quantitative Finance of Vienna University of Economics and Business. The club provides a platform for knowledge exchange and networking opportunities between students, alumni and the financial world. It is our aim to further strengthen the reputation and impact of the program in academia and the financial industry.

We believe that three factors make this program stand out globally:

  • The strong focus on mathematical theory and computer skills necessary to implement advanced financial models
  • A rigorous application process to select the brightest and most talented students.
  • World-class professors such as Prof. Kurt Hornik, member of the R-core developer team, Prof. Frey, co-author of "Quantitative Risk Management: Concepts, Techniques and Tools" and many more
  • Vienna as a Global Financial Centre for Central and Eastern Europe

What do we offer?

The main purpose of the club is to provide a platform for knowledge exchange and networking between students, alumni and members of the financial industry and academia. For our members we offer:

  • Regular casual meetings of students and alumni in Vienna.
  • Invited talks by industry experts and academic researchers on current topics within the quantitative finance community.
  • A database of all QFin members including contact information and current employment information.
  • A job board where companies and alumni can publish job postings exclusively to members of QFin.

For new students QFin organizes a Kick-Off event at the beginning of the winter term each year. This allows the new cohort of students to benefit from the experiences and lessons learned by their predecessors and to connect with our network from the very beginning.

For the local and global financial industry we offer:

  • An efficient way to promote job postings exclusively to the elite pool of our students and alumni. We welcome all level of job vacancies: internship positions, master thesis projects, entry level jobs as well as senior and specialist roles.
  • The possibility to become an industry partner of Quantitative Finance Club at an attractive rate. This is a unique opportunity to get in touch with some of the most talented students in the field of quantitative finance.
  • The chance to promote your company by publicly being associated with the talent pool of the Quantitative Finance Club.

Who can join QFin?

As the alumni club of the MSc Quantitative Finance we are open to all current and former students of the program, as well as to the students of its predecessor and the VGSF PhD students Upon decision of the board, the club can give membership to high profile individuals of the academic sciences or the financial industry.

About the Program

The MSc Quantitative Finance is a specialized master degree program offered by the Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU). The program presents an advanced education in the field of quantitative finance. It is an academically rigorous and demanding course with a clear mathematical orientation. With its excellent faculty, the challenging curriculum and a strict selection process the MSc Quantitative Finance is a world-class degree. For more information see the official program website.

Our Mission

Every year the master program in Quantitative Finance brings a group of up to 60 brilliant students to Vienna. Due to the size and structure of the program the students within one year of study are already well connected. We aim to leverage this potential by building a bridge between all classes of current and previous years.

Bringing together the knowledge and experience of more than a decade of quantitative finance alumni will create value for our current students, as well as for our alumni and the program as a whole. The club will enhance the study experience of our students and reinforce their identification with the program by providing a platform for lifelong networking.

The club is determined to strengthen the brand name MSc Quantitative Finance in the financial and academic world, both locally and around the globe. Through the exchange of knowledge, networking and job postings we want to further develop the reputation and impact of the program in academia and the financial industry.

The club should act in the interest of the students and work closely with the program directors. Another goal is to constantly improve the program to ensure its high level of recognition worldwide.
