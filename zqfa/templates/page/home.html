Welcome to QFin Club

Who we are and what we do

The Quantitative Finance Club (QFin Club for short) is a club for alumni and current students of the Master program Quantitative Finance of Vienna University of Economics and Business. The club provides a platform for knowledge exchange and networking opportunities between students, alumni and the financial world. It is our aim to further strengthen the reputation and impact of the program in academia and the financial industry.

WHat do we offer?

Knowledge exchange and networking
Casual meetups

We organize regular casual meetings with current students and alumni in Vienna

Access to Alumni network

We maintain a database of all QFin members including contact information and current employment

Job board

Exclusive job postings for our students by our alumni and partner companies

Talks by industry experts

Invited talks by industry experts and academic researchers on current topics within the quantitative finance community

Who can join?

Become a QFin Club Member

As the alumni club of the MSc Quantitative Finance we are open to all current and former students of the program, as well as to the students of its predecessor and the VGSF PhD students Upon decision of the board, the club can give membership to high profile individuals of the academic sciences or the financial industry.
